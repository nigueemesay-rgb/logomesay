Department

Pharmacy Department is a critical clinical and
administrative unit within a healthcare system responsible for
the safe, effective, and rational use of medications.
It functions as the central hub for
procurement, storage, compounding, dispensing, and distribution of pharmaceuticals,
while ensuring compliance with national regulatory standards and institutional policies.
The department plays a key role in
promoting patient safety, optimizing therapeutic outcomes, and
supporting broader public health objectives..
